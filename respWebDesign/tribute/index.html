Pohang's Famous Hand Rises From the Water

Information about Homigot

  • Homigot Sunrise Park is located about 30 minutes from Pohang
  • Being the easternmost point in mainland South Korea, Homigot is famous for having South Korea's earliest sunrise of the New Year.
  • The hand sculpture is called "Saengsaeng's hand" and was added to the park in 1999.
  • The sculpture represents the world coming together in harmony.
  • In addition to the sculpture the park has a lighthouse museum, and a giant open area for families to spend the day.
  • In the future, Homigot will have an aqaurium and be one of the largest attractions along the easter coast of South Korea.
